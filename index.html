Privacy Policy / Gizlilik Politikası

1. Data We Collect / Toplanan Veriler

LottoAI collects the following data:

— Account information (email address) when you sign in with email or Google

— Saved coupon data (stored on your device and optionally synced to our servers)

— Notification preferences (stored on your device)

— AI Assistant chat history (processed temporarily, not stored permanently)

— In-app preferences (name, theme, etc.)

— Push notification token (stored on our servers for notification delivery)

---

LottoAI aşağıdaki verileri toplar:

— Push bildirim token'ı (bildirim gönderimi için sunucuda saklanır)

Collected data is used solely to provide and improve app functionality. Your data is never shared with or sold to third parties.
